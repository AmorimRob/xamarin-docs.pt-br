* SystemConfiguration

* UIKit

* CoreMotion

* EventKitUI

* iAd
* LocalAuthentication
* MapKit
* MessageUI

* NetworkExtension
* NewsstandKit
* NotificationCenter
* PassKit
* Fotos
* PhotosUI
* PushKit
* QuickLook
* ReplayKit
* SafariServices
* Social
* Twitter
* VideoToolbox
* WatchConnectivity
* WatchKit
* WebKit
